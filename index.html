About Me

I am a PhD Research Scholar at IIT Ropar specializing in Algorithmic and Structural Graph Theory. My research focuses on the structural decomposition of restricted graph classes (e.g., chordal, interval, distance-hereditary, and P4-sparse graphs) to design efficient exact algorithms for NP-hard problems. I explore graph parameters like treewidth to develop parameterized algorithms for domination, combinatorial games, and coloring problems.
