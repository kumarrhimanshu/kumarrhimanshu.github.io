About Me

I am a PhD Research Scholar at IIT Ropar specializing in Algorithmic and Structural Graph Theory. My focus lies in designing efficient algorithms for NP-hard problems through structural decomposition (e.g., chordal, interval, and P4-sparse graphs) and parameterized complexity.
